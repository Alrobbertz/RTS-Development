About Rowing Telemetry Systems

A brief description...

Rowing Telemetry Systems (RTS) is an investigation into the possibility of producing low-cost instrumented oarlocks for competitive racing shells. Affordable instrumented oarlocks, accessible to High School and College level clubs would provide a significant competitive advantage. The RTS team strives to meet the following goals,

  • Oarlocks must sell for significantly less than any competition, the team is motivated produce an oarlock that can be retailed for a tenth of the average competition price.
  • Create a new force measuring architecture to mechanically isolate forces perpendicular to the oar shaft, providing significantly more precise force values.
  • Integrate an angle sensor accurate to a quarter of a degree, capable of handling 5+ million cycles.
  • Provide novel metrics, giving coaches and rowers tangible value to gauge progress.
  • Create an oarlock that will be significantly easier to install and operate; reducing installation time to under 1 minute by eliminating the need to calibrate.
  • Considerably more visually pleasing than any instrumented oarlock on the market, creating a product that looks to be designed for instrumentation in mind.

Prototype Oarlock Design

The purpose of this website is to facilitate the widespread testing of prototype oarlocks by providing a platform for rudimentary stroke analysis and data collection. All data collected through this website will be evaluated with the goal of continually improving hardware, firmware and software.
